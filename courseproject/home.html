About TOP TEN

Hello and Welcome to the TOP TEN website. TOP TEN is a website dedicated to discussion of the TOP TEN everything. Our lists include movies, video games, songs and so much more. This website survives due to our wonderful fanbase and user submissions. Without the ever growing content and community this website would cease to exist. So what are you waiting for? Have the perfect idea for a TOP TEN list? Let us know and we'll help you get started on becoming a paid author through TOP TEN.

How to submit Lists

In order to receive an application form please use the 'Contact' page to submit your request for an application. We will review your request and provide you with the steps for application. Once an application is submitted it will be reviewed by our team. Once we confirm the list meets the requirments we will reach out to verify your payment information so that we may provide you the one time payment of $100. Once the payment is confirmed the rights of your submission are transfered to TOP TEN. Below we will list our submissions quidelines to help you get started but please read over the application pdf that you will receive via email in it's entirity for our ful list of requirements

Submission Guidelines

  • The list must be your own creation (absolutely no plagerism).
  • Your list must contain atleast 2,000 words which includes the introduction.
  • Your list must contain 10 items, no more or no less.
  • You list must contain verifable quality sources with links to all sources used.
  • Proper formatting of the list is required and we will not accept lists that have spelling or gramatical errors.
  • Make sure to read the Submission Guide in it's entirity before submitting your work.
